INDULGE THE TASTE

UYED

- made of finely-grated corn that’s mixed with ground meat (beef or pork), and minced fish

DIBANG-DIBANG

- known as flying fish, a popular ingredient in Batanes cuisine

VUNES

- own version of Bicol’s famous laing. It is made of preserved gabi (taro) leaves and stalks cooked with cream, garlic, pork and patola (sponge gourd).

PAYI

- a highly prized seafood in Batanes cuisine, and is often served grilled or boiled with a side of garlic butter or vinegar dipping sauce.

WAKAY

- sweet potato

DUKAY

- yam

UVI

- ube (another type of yam)

SUDI

- Taro

LUNIS

- a traditional food in Batanes made from ground pork, taro leaves, and spices that are mixed together and formed into small logs.

SUPAS

- rice cooked in turmeric pulp (yellow ginger) that’s often mixed with garlic and pork.

TAMIDUK

- also called as pako or Fiddlehead fern sautéd and seasoned with salt, garlic and onions.

UTUT NA BULYAS

- scallions or green onions that are sautéed together with scrambled egg and spices.

GRILLED ARAYU

- also called mahi-mahi or golden dorado, this fish is indeed of golden color and slightly mixed with hues of blue and green.

KANANIS

- fried octopus, a favorite when it comes to beer drinking sessions.

SEAWEED SOUP

UNI

- Sea urchins that can be eaten raw or cooked.

DUMAY

- also called as a ‘common periwinkle’, is a specie of small edible sea snail that is boiled with seasonings.

COCONUT CRAB

- an endangered specie in Batanes but locals consider it a cuisine.

CHAYI

- also known as Spanish lime; not actually lime but more of a variant of lychee.

PALEK

- native wine made from sugarcane.

Official Government

Batanes is headed by Provincial Governor Marilou Cayco and Vice Governor Ignacio Villa. The Batanes Provincial Board, also known as the Sangguniang Panlalawigan, serves as its provincial legislature. The presidents of the provincial chapters of the Association of Barangay Captains, the Councilors' League, and the provincial president of Sangguniang Kabataan are also ex officio members.
